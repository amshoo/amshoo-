## 👋 About Me

I'm a software engineer, researcher, and technology leader with international experience across **Nigeria and China**. Currently pursuing a **PhD in Software Engineering** at Northwestern Polytechnic University (Xi'an, China), with a research focus on **Intelligent Transportation Systems (ITS)**, **Federated Learning**, and **Communication Optimization**.

I co-founded **[Allinnovat Tech Lab](https://github.com/amshoo)** — a technology lab delivering software solutions and innovation-driven digital products across web, mobile, and data platforms.

My work sits at the intersection of:
- 🚗 Smart mobility & Intelligent Transportation Systems
- 🔒 Privacy-preserving AI (Federated Learning)
- ⚡ Edge intelligence (TinyML)
- 🕸️ Complex Network Analysis
